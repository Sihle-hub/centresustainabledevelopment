ABOUT US

Get to know who we are and how we work

At CSD, we think of ourselves as ‘thinking partners’ and ‘critical friends’. Ideally, we would like to hear about your programme when it’s still just an idea… your sector-specific knowledge and experience are paramount, and, merged with our insights, we believe that together we can build your programme to be as efficient, effective, measurable, and sustainable, as possible.

Our approach to the way we work is underscored by

  • How we are trained and experienced – our team is schooled in social science and management accounting, with more than 20 years of cumulative experience, and we believe this enhances how we think about and approach our work;
  • How we collaborate – we value interdisciplinarity and regularly team-up with associates and collaborators;
  • How we can transfer our skills, expertise and insights – we can relate and apply these to a number of different contexts and sectors;
  • How we engage - we meet our clients where they are at… a high-rise office block, a food garden, a classroom, etc.; and
  • How we add our value - we strive to live our values proposition of providing quality support and services, on time, at standard, without waste, and with joy.

We are proud to have many repeat clients, which is testimony to our belief in cultivating meaningful and long-term relationships with the organisations we work with.

OUR SERVICES

    We develop comprehensive monitoring & evaluation (M&E) frameworks that align the project/organisational activities, outputs and outcomes, and measurement instruments to the reporting requirements.

  • MONITORING SUPPORT

    Developing Databases and matrices for measuring performance; this includes developing an integrated and interactive Performance Dashboard to track, analyse, illustrate, and highlight programme performance and key metrics that provide timely feedback to the project team for assimilation, programme management, and reporting.
